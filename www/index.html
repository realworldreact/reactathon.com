About

Reactathon is a week of events to educate and inspire newcomers to the React ecosystem.

  • Conference

    A full day educational conference designed to introduce you to the React ecosystem. Learn from the experts as they present case studies, best practices, and and lessons learned from years of working with React.

  • Workshops

    Taught by industry-leaders and core contributors, we offer a full week of workshops catering to beginners and advanced alike.

  • Hiring Mixer

    Meet the SF Bay’s top companies who use React for their front-end or are looking to make the switch, and find your next job.

  • Hackathon

    Put into practice the skills you’ve learned throughout the week in a 2-day hackathon. Compete for prizes and glory or code along with mentors and sponsors on guided projects.
